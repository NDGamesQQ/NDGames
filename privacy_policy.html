Privacy Policy for STELLAR IMPACT

Last updated: January 2025

Data Collection

STELLAR IMPACT does not collect, store, or share any personal data from its users. The game operates entirely offline and does not require any login, registration, or internet connection.

Information We Don't Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Usage statistics
  • Analytics data

Local Storage

The game stores your high scores and settings locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

STELLAR IMPACT does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Our game is safe for children as we do not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify users by updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
